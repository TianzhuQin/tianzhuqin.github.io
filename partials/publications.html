Selected Publications

Scholarship at the nexus of causal inference and sociotechnical systems

  1. Hug Fans or Follow Celebrities? How Nationalism Is Reinforced on Chinese Social Media

    Jean Hong, Kim Yong, Han Zhang, Tianzhu Qin (co-first author), Science Advances, forthcoming.

    Investigates cross-platform discourse dynamics and introduces a reproducible pipeline for impulse response analysis, powered by the open-source var-irf package I authored (GitHub · pip install var-irf).

  2. LLM-Driven Agent-Based Modeling for Information Network Simulation

    Tianzhu Qin, Working Paper & Open-Source Toolkit, 2024.

    Releases the llm-society package (GitHub · pip install llm-society) to simulate information diffusion with large language model agents, enabling policy stress-testing across networked societies.
